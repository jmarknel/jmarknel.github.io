What to expect

You're welcome to join us in North Austin on Sunday morning. We worship the Lord through singing, praying, praising and speaking our enjoyment of Christ. We meet at Brykerwoods Elementary school cafeteria. We also reserve classrooms for young people activities and the gym for toddlers.

You can be there at 10am for refreshments or just come at 10:30 for the start of worship service. We encourage everyone to bring a Bible.

The worship service will close at 11:45am. Some folks will go out to lunch.

You're free to park in the school parking lot or in the surrounding neighborhood.

Need Help?

Parking
