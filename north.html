Join us in north Austin

You're welcome to join us in North Austin on Sunday morning. We worship the Lord through singing, praying, praising and speaking our enjoyment of Christ.

What to expect

We meet at Brykerwoods Elementary school cafeteria. We also reserve classrooms for young people activities and the gym for toddlers.

You can be there at 10am for refreshments or just come at 10:30 for the start of worship service. We encourage everyone to bring a Bible.

The worship service will close at 11:45am. Some folks will go out to lunch.

You're free to park in the school parking lot or in the surrounding neighborhood.

Need Help?

If you need help in doing so please contact us at: (512) 443-0078

Parking

Parking is available in front of and behind the school and in the nearby neighborhood.
