Welcome

"Come to Me all who toil and are burdened, and I will give you rest.”

Matthew 11:28

As the church in Austin we have found the Lord’s word in Matthew 11:28 to be completely our experience and it is our joy and endeavor to help bring others into that same rest we have found.

Learn more about us

Christ is the unique foundation upon which we can build every part of our lives.

We invite you to join us in laying the proper foundation to our lives. One that will issue in the building up of the Body of Christ for the accomplishment of God’s eternal purpose.

Below are the various meetings that the church participates in.

Church meetings

Sunday mornings we meet in North, North West, Central, South and South West Austin.

Campus meetings

Meetings for college students on different campuses.

If you would like to get connected with other Christians on the University of Texas campus please contact us at: (512) 443-0078

Home meetings

Meetings in the different believers homes in Austin.

Conferences & trainings

These are extra-local meetings that the church participates in for the blending and building up of all the churches worldwide.
